## The Psychology of Being Late

### Why You Keep Doing It

1. **FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)**
   - You see green candles and panic buy
   - Your brain releases dopamine on "opportunity"
   - You're emotionally compromised

2. **Confirmation Bias**
   - You want to believe the pump will continue
   - You ignore bearish signals
   - You trust "influencers" who are already positioned

3. **Social Proof**
   - "Everyone is talking about it"
   - "It must be good if 10K people bought"
   - Herd mentality = bad entries

4. **Recency Bias**
   - You remember the last pump that worked
   - You forget the 10 pumps that didn't
   - Every situation feels "different this time"
